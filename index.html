EVENT SCORE

this is the inside of the EVENT SCORE. the CONTENT. the EVENT SCORE is a FLUXUS practice that dictates instructions (a score) for a piece of performance ART. sometimes the performance is performable but sometimes it is more about the feeling of the performance or even the poetry of the instructions that make the ART piece. EVENT SCORES are relatable to dadaist and surrealist practices.

look beneath this paragraph to see an example of a simple EVENT SCORE
